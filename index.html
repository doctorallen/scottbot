Scottbot

Scottbot is really just a couple of slash commands that respond with fun stuff.

/twss will send a "That's what she said" image to the room.

/mscott will send a random Michael Scott quote to the room.

Privacy

We don't collect or store any information. We listen for a command and respond appropriately. Nothing more. See for yourself at GitHub.
